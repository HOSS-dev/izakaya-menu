About Izakaya

🏮 What is an Izakaya?

An izakaya (居酒屋) is a Japanese pub where you order food and drinks casually throughout the evening — similar to tapas-style dining. Share dishes with the table and enjoy at your own pace!

📋 How to Order

① Browse the menu, set quantity with −/+, tap Add.
② Tap View Order at the bottom to review.
③ Tap Place Order and show the screen to staff.
④ To ask for the bill: Okaikei onegaishimasu!

💬 Useful Phrases

Sumimasen!Excuse me! (call staff)
Kore kudasai.I'll have this, please.
Okawari kudasai.One more, please.
Kanpai!Cheers! 🍻
Oishii!Delicious!
Okaikei onegaishimasu.Check, please.
